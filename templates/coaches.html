Meet Our Coaches


Ben Goldberg

Ben is a rising junior at Cornell University. He debated for Half Hollow Hills High School, where he qualified to the TOC in his senior year. Ben enjoys watching Netflix, sleeping, and listening to classical rock. He has expressed his distaste towards quarantine. When asked about his feelings towards Mongtomery Blair, he said, "Blair is the best team on the planet and I love coaching them." He also clearly expressed his favoritism for the best debater on the team, Eric Chen.

David Kinane

David is a rising sophomore at the University of Michigan. He debated for Unionville High School, and qualified to the TOC with a career total of 6 bids. He broke to eliminations at the TOC in his senior year, where he also won a speaker award. He enjoys listening to music, skateboarding, and surfing. In his free time, he cooks pineapples for breakfast and boils ice cubes in the hot tub.
